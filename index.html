Abou Us

At Prima German Language School, we are dedicated to achieving high rates of success in learning the German language by implementing effective and enjoyable modern teaching techniques.

Our team of Nigerian and native German tutors boasts a proven track record in teaching German as a foreign language, ensuring the successful completion of your targeted language levels.

We provide comprehensive training and guidance to empower our students to excel in their careers and seamlessly integrate into the German skilled workers market and meet various university-level language requirements.

Prima offers an ideal learning environment in the heart of FCT Abuja, equipped with a reliable power supply and internet access for your studies.

Tutoring sessions are conducted in small, friendly classes accommodating 4 to 10 students at the same language proficiency level. Our programs are designed to be hybrid, combining digital and face-to-face instruction.

We've also developed special digital tools to enhance your listening comprehension, speaking, writing, and reading skills in the German language. For beginners, our goal is to reach German Language level A1. Others will undergo standardized tests to determine their appropriate class placement.
